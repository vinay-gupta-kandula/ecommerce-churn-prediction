# Success Criteria Matrix

| Metric     | Minimum | Target | Stretch |
|-----------|--------|--------|---------|
| ROC-AUC   | 0.75   | 0.80   | 0.85    |
| Precision | 0.70   | 0.75   | 0.80    |
| Recall    | 0.65   | 0.70   | 0.75    |
| F1-Score  | 0.68   | 0.72   | 0.78    |

## Evaluation Notes
- ROC-AUC is prioritized as it measures overall ranking quality
- Precision is critical to reduce unnecessary retention costs
- Recall ensures high-risk churners are not missed
- Stretch goals indicate exceptional model performance
